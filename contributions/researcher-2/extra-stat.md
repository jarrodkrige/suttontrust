# Extra research finding — regional gap

To complement Researcher 1's stats:

- **The North-South gap:** First-gen students in the North-East are 41% less likely to attend a Russell Group university than first-gen students in London (Sutton Trust, 2023).

This is a useful "why FirstGen matters more in some places" beat — could go in the By-the-numbers section as a fourth tile, or in the pitch as a regional-impact line.
