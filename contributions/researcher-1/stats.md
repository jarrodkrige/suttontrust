# Research notes

Three statistics with sources:

- **~25%** of UK undergraduates are the first in their family to attend university (UCAS, 2023)
- **33%** higher first-year drop-out rate among first-generation students (Sutton Trust, 2022)
- **78%** of first-gen students wish they'd known someone "who'd been through it" before applying (Sutton Trust survey, 2024)

Together these point to a clear gap: peer mentoring + plain-English guidance.
