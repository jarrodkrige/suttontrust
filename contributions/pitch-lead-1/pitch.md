# FirstGen — 60-second team pitch

> One in four UK undergrads is the first in their family at university. They've got the grades. What they don't have is someone who's been through it — someone who can tell them what nobody told them.
>
> So we built FirstGen.
>
> A site that connects sixth-formers with current undergrads from similar backgrounds. Real students, real answers, no jargon.
>
> In the last hour, our team — coordinators, developers, writers, designers, an accessibility champion, researchers — used Microsoft Copilot and GitHub Copilot to build the marketing site you're seeing behind me.
>
> Nobody on this team had built a website before this hour. AI didn't replace any of us. It made every one of us a designer, a writer, a developer, all at once.
>
> That's what AI-native software development feels like. And we just shipped.
