# 🎨 Visual Designer 3

You're a third pair of hands on the imagery. VD-1 owns the hero. VD-2 owns secondary imagery (voice portraits, buddy photo, content-section icons). **Your job is the supporting visuals**: dividers, backgrounds, optional inline icons, alt hero variants the team can choose from.

## What lives in this folder
- `alt-heroes/` — alternative hero images, in case the team wants options
- `dividers/` — section dividers, decorative shapes
- `backgrounds/` — subtle background textures or shapes
- `style-notes.md` — what you'd standardise across the imagery (consistent line weight, palette, etc.)

## Coordinate with
- **Visual Designers 1 and 2** — agree style/mood in minute 3 so the whole page reads as one piece
- **Software Developer 1** (stylesheet steward) — share your palette additions
- **Copywriters** — your imagery should match their tone

## First 2 minutes
1. Sign in to copilot.microsoft.com with your role-card account.
2. Look at what VD-1 is making. Match the style.
3. Try:
   > "In the same warm pastel illustration style as <reference>, generate three subtle section divider shapes — soft curves, no hard lines. Web-friendly aspect."
4. Save your favourite as `dividers/wave-1.png` in this folder.

## Prompts that work
- *"Same style as <paste reference>, but a different subject."*
- *"Three subtle background patterns inspired by this image — no detail, just colour and texture."*
- *"What single small visual element would tie this hero to this footer image?"*

## House rule
**Coherence over novelty.** Your job is to make VD-1 and VD-2's work feel inevitable, not to add a new style of your own.
