# Wildcard proposal — "What nobody told me" FAQ

We've got the personas, the features, the stats. But the team kept saying "what nobody told me" — that's the **emotional hook**. Let's add a small FAQ-style section between What-you-find and By-the-numbers.

## Proposed structure

```html
<section id="nobody-told-me">
  <h2>What nobody told me</h2>
  <p class="lede">The stuff you wish someone had said in week one.</p>
  <ul class="faq">
    <li><strong>Office hours aren't an appointment.</strong> Just turn up. They're literally for you.</li>
    <li><strong>You're allowed to ask for help.</strong> Personal tutors exist for exactly this.</li>
    <li><strong>Everyone feels lost in week one.</strong> Including the people who look like they don't.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
```

If the agent decides the section flow is already full, drop this one. But it would be a memorable beat in the pitch.
