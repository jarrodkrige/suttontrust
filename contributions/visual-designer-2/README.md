# 🎨 Visual Designer 2

You partner with Visual Designer 1. Split the work — they take the hero, you take secondary imagery (illustrations for the "how it helps" cards, an icon set, a closing image). Or another split — agree in minute 3.

## What lives in this folder
- `secondary-images/` — supporting images for content sections
- `icons/` — small graphics for the feature list
- `closing-image.png` — a quieter image for the CTA section
- `style-notes.md` — your visual rules so you and VD1 stay coherent

## Coordinate with
- **Visual Designer 1** — agree style/mood in minute 3
- **Visual Designers 3 & 4** (if present) — they own dividers/backgrounds and the closing image; coordinate the palette
- **Software Developer 1** (stylesheet steward) — they style around your imagery
- **Copywriters** — your imagery should match their tone

## First 2 minutes
1. Sign in to copilot.microsoft.com with your role-card account.
2. Look at what VD1 is generating, or chat to them.
3. Try:
   > "Three small illustration icons in matching warm pastel style: one for 'connect with a buddy', one for 'practical guides', one for 'real undergrads'. Flat illustration, friendly, simple shapes. Same colour palette across all three."

## Prompts that work
- *"Same style as `<paste reference>`, but a different subject."*
- *"Tighter composition, less white space."*
- *"Make these three feel like a set — same colour palette, same line weight."*
- *"What colours would feel hopeful but not naive for a charity audience?"*
