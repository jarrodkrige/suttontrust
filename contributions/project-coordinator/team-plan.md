# FirstGen team plan

Decided as a team in the first 5 minutes:

## Section flow (top → bottom)
1. **Hero** — headline, tagline, primary CTA (Find your match)
2. **Who is this for?** — three personas (first-in-family, money-worried, "just want to talk")
3. **What you'll find here** — feature grid (4 tiles)
4. **By the numbers** — three stats from the researcher
5. **Voices** — student quotes
6. **Sign up** — waitlist CTA

## Tone
Warm, friendly, plain English. Not corporate. Address the reader as "you".

## Roles in play
- 1 PC (me), 4 Devs, 5 Copywriters, 4 Designers, 2 Pitch Leads, 2 A11y, 2 Researchers
- Devs own the shell, Copywriters own the words, Designers own the look, A11y owns the floor (contrast, headings)
