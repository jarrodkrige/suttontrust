# 🎨 Visual Designer 4

You're the fourth designer — extra capacity. VD-1 hero, VD-2 secondaries, VD-3 supporting visuals. **Your job is the closing image and the wildcard**: the warmest, most personal image on the site (the moment that makes a reader feel something), or whatever the team is missing visually.

## What lives in this folder
- `closing-image.png` — a quiet, warm image for the final CTA section (e.g. two students chatting in a courtyard)
- `wildcards/` — bolder ideas you'd ship if the team has appetite (a mascot, a pattern set, a single illustrated portrait)
- `style-notes.md` — what makes the imagery feel cohesive

## Coordinate with
- **Visual Designers 1, 2, 3** — read their style notes first; match the palette and line weight
- **Copywriter 4 / Pitch Lead** — they own the closing CTA copy; your image should resonate with it
- **Accessibility Champion** — they'll write alt text for whatever you produce

## First 2 minutes
1. Sign in to copilot.microsoft.com with your role-card account.
2. Look at what VD-1 has made. Note the palette, line weight, mood.
3. Try:
   > "Warm, hopeful illustration: two UK sixth-form students sat together on a campus bench, looking at a phone, smiling. Same pastel illustration style as <paste reference>. Editorial, not stock-photo."
4. Iterate. Save the winner as `closing-image.png`.

## Prompts that work
- *"Same scene, but the students are laughing this time."*
- *"Same style, totally different scene — a student waving from a window."*
- *"Critique this image for a charity microsite — is the warmth real or performative?"*

## House rule
**The closing image is the last thing the audience sees.** Make it the warmest, most human one. The site's promise lives here.
