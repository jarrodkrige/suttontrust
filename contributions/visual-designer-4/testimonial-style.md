# Testimonial card style

For the Voices section. Each quote should look like a sticky-note pulled out of a journal:

- Background: `#FFF7EE` (very pale warm — softer than coral)
- Text: ink `#1A1A2E`
- 1px ink border at 15% opacity
- 12px corner radius
- Subtle drop shadow (offset 0,2px, blur 8px, ink at 8% opacity)
- Quote mark (large, coral) in the top-left, half off the card
- Attribution in italic Inter, smaller (14px), trust-coloured

The vibe: warm, editorial, "real letter from a friend".
