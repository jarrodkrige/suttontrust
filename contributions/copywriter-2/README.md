# ✍️ Copywriter 2

You partner with Copywriter 1 to write the site's words. Split the work — they take hero + CTA, you take the body sections (the "what / who / how" content). Or split a different way; agree in minute 3.

## What lives in this folder
- `body-copy.md` — text for the content sections
- `microcopy.md` — small bits: nav labels, button text, footer line
- `voice-notes.md` — agreed voice rules so you and CW1 stay consistent

## Coordinate with
- **Copywriter 1** — they own hero + CTA; agree the voice in minute 3, share drafts as you go
- **Copywriters 3–5** (if present) — they polish, pitch alternatives, and own microcopy; check what they've drafted
- **Researchers 1 & 2** — pull in stats and real quotes they surface
- **Software Developers** — they consume your text
- **Accessibility Champion** — they review for clarity

## First 2 minutes
1. Sign in to copilot.microsoft.com with your role-card account.
2. Read what Copywriter 1 has dropped, or ping them.
3. Ask:
   > "I'm writing the body sections of FirstGen — a charity microsite for 16-18 year-olds who'd be first in their family at uni. Give me 3 short paragraphs (50 words each) for sections titled 'What it is', 'Who it's for', 'How it helps'. Warm, honest, no clichés."

## Prompts that work
- *"Rewrite this paragraph as if you're talking to one person, not a crowd."*
- *"This sounds corporate — make it feel like a sixth-former wrote it for a friend."*
- *"Compress to 30 words. Keep the strongest sentence."*
- *"Insert this stat from the Researcher naturally: <stat>"*
