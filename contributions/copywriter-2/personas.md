# Section 2 — Who is this for?

## Three personas

**You're the first in your family to apply.**
You've got the grades. You've got the offer. But everyone around you is googling "what is a personal tutor" too.

**You're worried about money.**
Maintenance loans, term dates, what £9k actually covers — it's a lot. We'll walk you through it in plain English.

**You just want to talk to someone who gets it.**
Not a brochure. A real undergrad, two years in, who remembers what it felt like.
