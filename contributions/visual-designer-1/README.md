# 🎨 Visual Designer 1

You produce the imagery. Hero images, illustrations, icons. You'll mostly use **Microsoft Copilot's image generator**.

## What lives in this folder
- `hero.png` — the main hero image (Software Developers will reference this filename)
- `secondary-images/` — supporting images for content sections
- `palette.md` — colour suggestions for the Stylesheet Steward (SWD-1)

## Coordinate with
- **Visual Designer 2** — agree style/mood in minute 3 so the site feels coherent
- **Visual Designers 3 & 4** (if present) — they make supporting visuals and the closing image; share your palette early so theirs match
- **Software Developer 1** (stylesheet steward) — palette + typography decisions
- **Copywriters** — your imagery should match the tone they're setting
- **Accessibility Champion** — they'll check your images have alt text and good contrast

## First 2 minutes
1. Sign in to copilot.microsoft.com with your role-card account.
2. Click the **image** option (or say "make me an image of...")
3. Try this prompt:
   > "Warm, hopeful illustration of three diverse UK sixth-form students looking at a university campus from a hill. Soft pastel colours, illustrative, not photorealistic. Editorial magazine style. Wide aspect ratio for a website hero."
4. Iterate. When you have a winner, save it as `hero.png` and upload to this folder.

## Prompts that work
- *"Make it warmer."* / *"Make it less corporate."* / *"Less stock-photo, more illustration."*
- *"Same scene, different angle."* / *"Same style, different subject — a student on a video call with a buddy."*
- *"Suggest a 4-colour palette inspired by this image."*
- *"Critique this image for a charity microsite — is it warm, honest, inclusive? What's off?"*
